About Weather Prediction Project

This research project explores the correlation between weather conditions and the feasibility of playing a tennis game. We employ the Random Forest algorithm for predictive analysis, taking into account various weather variables such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation.

Our goal is to assist tennis players and organizers in making informed decisions regarding game participation, ultimately enhancing the overall tennis experience. We've created a weather prediction tool to help you determine whether it's a good day to play tennis based on the weather conditions in your area.
